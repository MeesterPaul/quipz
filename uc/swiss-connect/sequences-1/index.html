Swiss Connect:
Sequences 1

by Paul Ratcliffe

Compatibility note: For reasons known only to Google at time of writing this does not work properly on a mobile phone (sorry).

Pick one of the four categories (they are Swiss German Jass suits, in case you're wondering) and try to figure out what comes fourth in the sequence with as few clues as possible, just like in round 2 of Only Connect. Tip: click on the three dots icon ⋮ and you can choose to view full screen. Plenty more puzzles from Paul on PuzzleTrail
